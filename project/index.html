Why this site?

It's hard to keep track of your life when it's happening to you. Habit breaks down your habits by looking at why you do what you do.

There are three parts to behavior: the antecedent, what happens before a behavior, behavior, the actual action that happens because of the antecednet; and the condition, the result that happens afterward to strengthen the behavior.

The main goal is to track what leads up to your desired and undesired behaviors so you can hack your own mental programming and get your logic brain leading you and not just your monkey one.
